Assignment 7: Autobiographical Game

Description

Dolma is an autobiographical game that delves into the experiences of navigating the millennial era through the fractured lens of identity, belonging, and memory.

This deeply personal game invites the player to engage in the reconstruction of a self-portrait, piecing together chaotic yet meaningful fragments of the protagonist's past.

In this specific game, the player assumes my role to explore my family tree, utilizing a reward/punishment system to better understand my personal journey.
