Artist Bio

Zhuoyu Zhang (she/they) is a new media artist who graduated with a BFA in Visual Communication from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and is pursuing an MFA in Design & Technology at Parsons School of Design. As a Gen Z artist under the Internet age, Zhuoyu navigates the complexities of digital culture. Immersed in a constant flow of viewpoints and narratives, she uses dynamic imagery, 3D modeling, and mixed media to critically analyze the intersection of past traumas and current societal challenges.
